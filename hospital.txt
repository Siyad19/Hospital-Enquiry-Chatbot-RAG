 St. Jude Memorial Hospital is a premier 500-bed tertiary care facility dedicated to providing compassionate and advanced healthcare.
Established in 1995, the hospital has consistently been ranked among the top medical institutions for patient safety and clinical excellence.
The campus includes a main inpatient tower, a dedicated outpatient pavilion, and a comprehensive research facility.

The Emergency Department operates 24/7 and is designated as a Level 1 Trauma Center, fully equipped to handle critical medical emergencies, cardiac events, and severe injuries.
It features a specialized pediatric emergency wing staffed by board-certified pediatricians and neonatal specialists.
The trauma team is supported by an on-site helipad for rapid air-ambulance transfers.

The hospital houses several centers of excellence, including the Comprehensive Cancer Center, the Heart and Vascular Institute, and the Advanced Neurology Center.
Each department utilizes state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging, such as 3T MRI and ultra-high-speed CT scanners, to ensure precise diagnoses.
Robotic-assisted surgery systems are also available for minimally invasive procedures in urology, gynecology, and general surgery.

General visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily, though restrictions may vary in intensive care units to ensure patient rest and recovery.
Outpatient clinics require prior appointments, which can be scheduled online via the patient portal or by calling the central helpline.
All visitors must check in at the main reception desk to receive a temporary security pass before entering inpatient wards.

St. Jude Memorial Hospital accepts a wide range of private insurance providers, Medicare, and Medicaid.
Financial counseling services are available on-site to assist families with payment plans, billing queries, and insurance pre-authorizations.
The billing department provides transparent pricing estimations for elective procedures upon request.
